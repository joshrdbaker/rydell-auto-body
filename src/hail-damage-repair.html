Free Hail Damage Estimates

Nobody enjoys a hail storm in our Grand Forks community, but what we dread most is the cost of the damage to our vehicles. Let our professional staff ease the pain by offering you a free hail damage estimate. The Rydell Collision Center works with all insurance companies to give you an accurate estimate and help you understand the repair process.

We bring your vehicle inside our facility, wash the exterior to better expose the damage, then mark dents with a washable marker so you can see the quantity and size of hail damage and decide whether to move forward with repairs.

We enter the details into digital estimating software according to your insurer’s guidelines. While the estimate takes time, we offer a free courtesy ride anywhere in Grand Forks and East Grand Forks city limits, or you can relax with complimentary coffee. Call 701-792-2846 to schedule your free hail damage assessment, or visit us at 1502 28th Avenue South, Grand Forks, ND 58201.

Hail Damage Repair Process

After your free estimate, we’ll schedule a day that works for you. Depending on severity, we may use Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) to remove dents without filler or paint. If damage is larger than about a quarter, we use conventional repair: metal work, a small amount of filler when needed, paint, and reassembly, followed by a complimentary express detail.

We guarantee our hail repairs for as long as you own the vehicle. Learn more about our collision center on Rydell Cars or call 701-792-2846 today.
